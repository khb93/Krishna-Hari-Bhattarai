Krishna Hari Bhattarai

Independent Consultant

Krish has dedicated himself in social service for last nine years, combating poverty and illiteracy in rural communities of Nepal. He earned his Master's degre in Business and Finance from Tribhuvan University and has been actively involved in service since 2015.

In 2015, Krish met with Magda Nos, a pioneering mountaineer and a passionate social worker from Spain whose dedication and guidance profoundly influenced his approach to serving under privileged communities. His way of looking at people and societies has changed ever since. He has been carrying out different social projects in Nepal in collaboration with many international non-profits. His role consists of identification of community needs, planning and development of projects, implementing and following up of numerous educational and community development programs in Tanahun, Sindhupalchowk, Kathmandu, Gorkha and Udayapur districts. His passion lies in pursuit of creating a long lasting, sustainable change in the lives of those in need.
